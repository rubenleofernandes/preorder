Synopsis

Elton Coleman, a bank accountant in Chicago, drowns in debt, lies, and despair. Behind the mask of a devoted husband and father, he hides mounting failures—until one night, his desperation takes a dark turn. He stages the scene, vanishes under a new name—James Park—and rebuilds his life in Bangkok with the help of a fixer. Eight years later, retired detective Winston Bell spots the cracks. As suspicion grows, James prepares for the worst, haunted by the life he buried. But the past doesn’t stay silent. And a man can change everything—except the weight of his own sins.

Ruben Leonard Fernandes

Ruben Leonard Fernandes is an author, poet, songwriter, music producer and software engineer. He is a student based in Bengaluru, India. While pursuing his engineering degree, he devoted his spare moments to crafting this thriller. His passion for storytelling began in childhood through poems and short stories, gradually shaping his creative voice. Alongside fiction, he is also a songwriter, blending emotion and narrative through music. With a love for cinematic storytelling, Ruben aims to create gripping, edge-of-the-seat experiences for readers.
